## What You'll Have at the End

A complete **Strategic AI Brief** (4-6 pages) integrating everything from the four-week series into a coherent strategic narrative — current state, 12-month plan, operating system, the strategic choice you're making, and how you'll measure progress. Plus a 100-day plan for the next quarter.

## How to Approach This Week

This week zooms out from project-level thinking to portfolio- and organization-level thinking. The frameworks here build directly on Weeks 1, 2, and 3 — your strategy brief, your pilot charter, and your people memo all feed the final synthesis on Day 28.

If you've done the work along the way, the synthesis is mostly assembly. If you haven't, this week reveals it. The capstone exercise on Day 28 is the test of whether the four weeks actually changed how you think.
