# 30-Day AI Strategy Roadmap

## The Arc

This isn't a random collection of AI topics. It's a deliberate progression:

**Week 1 (Foundations)** — Get your bearings. Understand what AI strategy actually is, assess where your org stands, and learn the frameworks that prevent expensive mistakes.

**Week 2 (Implementation)** — Pick your first initiative and do it right. Pilot design, team structure, technical decisions, and the metrics that actually matter.

**Week 3 (Organization)** — The hard part isn't technology — it's people. Talent strategy, change management, upskilling, and building a culture that can absorb AI.

**Week 4 (Scale)** — Go from one successful pilot to a portfolio of AI initiatives. Measurement frameworks, organizational design, and long-term competitive positioning.

## Week 1: Foundations

The goal this week is clarity. By Friday, you should be able to articulate what AI strategy means for your specific org — not in buzzwords, but in concrete decisions.

| Day | Topic | Key Takeaway |
|-----|-------|-------------|
| 01 | What AI Strategy Actually Means | Strategy is about choosing what NOT to do |
| 02 | The Build vs. Buy Decision | A decision matrix for your first fork in the road |
| 03 | Data Readiness Assessment | Honest assessment of where you actually stand |
| 04 | AI Use Case Prioritization | Pick the right first bet, not the exciting one |
| 05 | Governance Basics | Governance as competitive advantage, not compliance burden |
| 06 | The Vendor Landscape | Navigate without getting sold |
| 07 | Week 1 Reflection | Consolidate what you've learned, identify gaps |

## Week 2: Implementation

The goal this week is execution. By Friday, you should have a concrete pilot plan — scoped, staffed, measured, and with stakeholder buy-in.

| Day | Topic | Key Takeaway |
|-----|-------|-------------|
| 08 | Designing an AI Pilot That Actually Proves Value | Pilots need a hypothesis, a baseline, and a decision gate |
| 09 | Building the Right Team | Start with 3-5 people and a clear problem, not a full data science department |
| 10 | Technical Architecture Decisions | Design for throwaway, not permanence |
| 11 | Stakeholder Management & Expectation Setting | Map influence and support, then engage each quadrant differently |
| 12 | Success Metrics That Matter | If your metrics dashboard doesn't include a dollar figure, it's a science project |
| 13 | The Valley of Disillusionment | Every AI initiative hits a trough — survival depends on translation, not technology |
| 14 | Week 2 Reflection | Consolidate your plan with a pilot readiness checklist |

## How to Get the Most Out of This

1. **Do the work.** Each day has a "Your Move" section. Actually do it.
2. **Use the templates.** They're in `/templates`. Fill them out for your org.
3. **Discuss with others.** Strategy sharpens through debate, not solo reading.
4. **Be honest about where you are.** The readiness assessment on Day 03 only works if you're candid.
