## What You'll Have at the End

A one-page **People Strategy Memo** for your organization's AI work, structured around six honest questions about talent inventory, upskilling investment, change management, AI ethics evidence, team structure, and community of practice. Day 21 is the synthesis.

## How to Approach This Week

Week 3 is harder than Weeks 1 and 2 because the answers are less amenable to frameworks. The exercises this week require uncomfortable honesty about your organization's structural readiness for AI — not where you wish it were, where it actually is.

If you're going to skip exercises in this series, don't skip them this week. The people side is where most AI initiatives die.
